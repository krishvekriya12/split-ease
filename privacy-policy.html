Privacy Policy for SplitEase

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SplitEase ("the App") handles your information. SplitEase is developed and maintained by an individual developer.

Information We Collect

SplitEase does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. All data you enter into the app — including group names, member names, and expense records — is stored locally on your device only, using local on-device storage.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since no data leaves your device, SplitEase does not share any information with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services. The App does not contain ads or analytics tracking.

No Account Required

SplitEase does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal identifying information to use the App.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's storage from your device settings.

Children's Privacy

SplitEase does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13, as no data is collected at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future (for example, if new features requiring data collection are added), this page will be updated accordingly, and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.
